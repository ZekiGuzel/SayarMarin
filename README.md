## 🛥️ About Sayar Marin

Sayar Marin is a premium maritime service company founded by **Burak Sayar** in Marmaris, Turkey. With over 5 years of hands-on experience in yacht restoration, painting, and structural repair, we have proudly served 150+ vessels across the Turkish Riviera and Mediterranean Sea.

We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to deliver results that meet the highest international standards — because your yacht deserves nothing less.

---

## 🔧 Our Services

### 🎨 Hull Painting
Complete hull preparation and painting using premium marine-grade paints. We apply professional anti-fouling treatments that protect your vessel against marine growth, corrosion, and osmosis — ensuring optimal performance and longevity in Mediterranean waters.

### 🏗️ Superstructure Painting
Flawless topside and superstructure finishes that restore your yacht's original beauty. We use two-part polyurethane and epoxy systems for a durable, high-gloss finish that withstands UV exposure and harsh sea conditions.

### 🔩 Structural Repairs
Expert fiberglass lamination, gelcoat repairs, and structural reinforcement. From minor surface scratches to major impact damage, our technicians assess and restore your vessel's structural integrity with precision.

### 🌊 Osmosis Treatment
Comprehensive osmosis diagnosis, treatment, and prevention. We dry the hull completely, remove all blistering, apply epoxy barrier coats, and seal the hull to prevent future moisture ingress — extending your vessel's life significantly.

### 🪵 Teak Deck Work
Installation, repair, and complete renewal of traditional and synthetic teak decks. We restore worn teak to showroom condition using premium caulking compounds and finishing oils — beautiful and non-slip.

### ✨ Detailing & Polishing
Full exterior detailing packages including oxidation removal, compounding, multi-stage machine polishing, and ceramic coating application. We bring your yacht's surfaces back to a brilliant mirror finish.

### 🔌 Electrical & Systems
Inspection and repair of onboard electrical systems, navigation lights, bilge pumps, and battery systems. We ensure all critical systems are functioning safely and efficiently.

### 🧰 General Maintenance
Seasonal lay-up and recommissioning services, engine room cleaning, seacock servicing, and full pre-sale inspections. We keep your yacht in peak condition year-round.

### 🚨 Emergency Repairs
24/7 emergency repair services for urgent situations. Whether you're in the marina or out at sea, our team responds quickly to get you back on the water safely.
